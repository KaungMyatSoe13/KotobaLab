# 🎌 Japanese Vocabulary Quiz

A beautiful, interactive web application for practicing Japanese vocabulary with audio pronunciation. Upload your own vocabulary list and test yourself with this engaging quiz interface.

## 💡 Motivation

As a Japanese language learner living alone, I faced a common problem: I had no one to quiz me on vocabulary. I would write down words in my notebook for practice, but I needed someone to read the pronunciations aloud so I could practice both listening and writing simultaneously.

This web application solves that exact problem! It acts as your personal vocabulary tutor, pronouncing words for you while you practice writing and recall. Whether you're studying alone at home or on the go, this tool helps you:

- **Listen** to proper pronunciation
- **Write** the Japanese characters
- **Recall** the meanings
- **Practice** independently without needing a study partner

Born from personal necessity, this app is designed for self-learners who want to improve their Japanese vocabulary skills through active listening and writing practice.

## Features

- **Excel File Upload** - Import your vocabulary lists from `.xlsx` or `.xls` files
- **Audio Pronunciation** - Listen to Japanese pronunciations using text-to-speech
- **Randomized Order** - Words are shuffled for better learning
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Quick navigation with keyboard controls
- **Fully Responsive** - Works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Session Persistence** - Your progress is saved during your browser session
- **Modern UI** - Beautiful gradient design with smooth animations
- **Template Download** - Download an Excel template to get started quickly

## 📊 Excel File Format

Your Excel file should have the following columns:

| Pronunciation | Japanese Writing | Meaning |
|---------------|------------------|---------|
| konnichiwa    | こんにちは       | Hello   |
| arigatou      | ありがとう       | Thank you |
| sayounara     | さようなら       | Goodbye |

**Column Names:** (case-insensitive)
- `Pronunciation` - Romaji pronunciation
- `Japanese Writing` - Japanese characters (hiragana/katakana/kanji)
- `Meaning` - English translation

💡 **Tip:** Click "Download Template Excel" in the app to get a pre-formatted template!

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `Enter` or `Space` | Reveal answer / Next word |
| `R` | Replay audio |
| `H` | Hide revealed answer |

## How to Use

1. **Upload Your Vocabulary File**
   - Click the upload area and select your Excel file
   - Or download the template and fill it with your vocabulary

2. **Start the Quiz**
   - Click "Start Quiz" to begin
   - Words will be shuffled randomly

3. **Practice**
   - Listen to the pronunciation
   - Try to recall the meaning
   - Click "Reveal" to check your answer
   - Click "Next Word" to continue

4. **Complete**
   - Finish all words to see the completion message
   - Click "Go Back to Home" to start a new session
